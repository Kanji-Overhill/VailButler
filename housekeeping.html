Housekeeping

Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest quality, professional and trustworthy cleaning service to our customers homes in the Vail Valley. Cleaning is a very personal offering, that is why we tailor our services and listen carefully to what your property needs. This way we can not only meet your expectations but exceed them every time.

What we offer?

Our 25-point checklist is our signature Vail Valley Cleaning service that will ensure your house is spotless. In our uniquely designed checklist we cover everything from window cleaning and fan dusting to linen and towel washing.

CHECKLIST

Kitchen

· Appliance exteriors cleaned
· Microwave exterior/interior cleaned
· Countertops and backsplash disinfected (all items removed and replaced)
· Cabinet fronts cleaned
· Sinks cleaned and disinfected (dishes upon request)
· Trash emptied
· Windowsills cleaned (blinds dusted upon request)
· Floor vacuumed or washed

Bedroom

· Beds made (linens changed and washed upon request)
· Furniture dusted front and behind
· Windowsills cleaned
· Floors vacuums and/or disinfected
· Mirrors cleaned

Bathroom

· Tub and shower tiles scrubbed and disinfected
· Toilets disinfected inside and out
· Countertops disinfected
· Sink scrubbed and disinfected
· Floors vacuumed and/or washed

Other living areas

· Furniture vacuumed
· Ceiling fans dusted
· Furniture organized and straightened
· Fingerprints removed
· Floors, dusted washed or vacuumed
· Stairways, dusted washed or vacuumed
· Windows cleaned
· Trash emptied
· Hallways and stairways

Our products

Although cleaning your home is very important to us, we also have a responsibility to our plant and environment. We make sure that everything we use is not only eco-friendly but also safe and effective for your home.

Here are some of the ways we contribute our environment:

· Use reusable buckets and bottles.
· Reduce paper waste by online scheduling.
· Use safe, effective cleaning products i.e. all of our products are certified non-toxic, biodegradable and we do no use any dyes or scented formulas.
· Use washable and reusable microfiber rags that capture allergens better that disposable paper.

Policies/Terms

Cancellation Policy
We know life can get busy. So if it necessary to cancel or skip your scheduled service, Vail Butler requires a 3 business day notice. If we receive less than a 3 day business notice, we will have to charge you the full amount of your cleaning.

Payment
Full payment is due at the time of your cleaning.
We accept checks and all major credit cards.

Arrival Times
Our hours of operations are between 8:00am and 4:00 pm

Let us make it right
We know cleaning can be a very personal and human service. At Vail Butler we realize that sometimes an area may not be cleaned to your satisfaction. Vail butler will happily re-clean any area of the house if notified within 24 hours of our initial cleaning.

Breakage or loss
Vail Butler is fully insured. Our liability limits are set at a max of 10 times the cost of the service on the day in which the breakage or loss occurred. However, notification must be made within 24 hours of service. If notified after, we are not responsible or liable for any damage occurred.
