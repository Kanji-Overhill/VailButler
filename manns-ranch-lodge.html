Manns Ranch Home Minutes to Vail Village Lift

  • Entire home/apt
  • 7 Guests
  • 3 Bedrooms

This beautiful 3 bedroom 3 bathroom home is steps away from the bus stop and only minutes from the ski lifts. In the summer time, it is a short walk to Booth Creek Falls, one of the best hiking trails in Vail. Remodeled and professionally decorated, this townhome is one of the nicest in its neighborhood. It is very spacious and located on the sunny side of the Valley with great views of East Vail from the deck on the main floor.
This house in right on the bus route. It is the last stop the bus makes before arriving to Vail's Transportation Center (3-5 min drive). The bus is free and runs every 15 minutes during the winter.
